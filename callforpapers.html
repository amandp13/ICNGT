CALL FOR PAPERS


Call For Papers : PDF Version Is Available:  Click Here

The Conference aims to provide an active platform for research scientists, engineers, and practitioners throughout the world to present their latest research findings, ideas, and applications in the fields of interest which fall under the scope of IT. Prospective authors are invited to submit original research papers (not being considered for publication elsewhere) in standard conference template describing new theoretical and/or experimental research results in the following tracks (but are not limited to) :


Track-1: Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Robotics
Artificial & Augmented intelligence and their applications, Biometrics & RFID, Computational Intelligence, Deep Learning, Control Systems, Instrumentation and Measurement, Biomedical, Sensors and Sensing Systems, Robotics and Automation, Neural networks, Speech processing, Modelling & Simulation of Machines.

Track-2: Data Science and Analytics
Big Data Analysis, Data and Business analytics, Computational Social Science & Social Networks, Age of Information, Sentiment analysis, Knowledge Discovery & Data Mining

Track-3: Network, Mobile Computing and Internet of Things
5G and cloud computing, Cellular networks, Cross-layer optimization and control, WLAN, WPAN, RFID, and NFC, Datacenter networking, Energy efficiency in networks, Edge and fog computing/networking, Informationcentric networking, Internet architecture, Internet of Things, Medium access control, Multimedia networking, Network management, Network measurement and analysis, Network security and privacy, Network virtualization, Router and switch design, Routing and multicast, Software-defined networking, Vehicular networks.

Track-4: Security and Privacy
Information security and privacy, Security measures, Social networks and digital security, Device synchronization and protection, Digital piracy and security, Cyber security.

Track-5: Computer Vision and Biometrics
Biometrics and cyber security, Control Systems, Instrumentation and Measurement, Biomedical, Sensors and Sensing Systems, Robotics and Automation.

Track-6: Theory and Systems
Product Safety & Reliability Engineering, Social and Humanitarian Implications of Technology, Theoretical Computer Science, Emerging technologies and their applications in education, publications, tourism, healthcare, agriculture etc, Geoscience and Remote Sensing.
